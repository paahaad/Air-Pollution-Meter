gassensor we are using is MQ135 which can also detect the co2,smoke,NH3 etc.
you can refer datasheet of MQ135 for the more detail.

pin description:

1.VCC:
	power souce of +5v.

2.ground:
	connect the ground to the module(ardunio)

3.digital out:
	use to get the digital output.
4.analog out:
	use to get the analog output.
